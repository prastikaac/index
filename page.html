The Electronic Diversity Visa (EDV) Program

The Diversity Visa Program has been running successfully since 1994, and every year, many participants come a little closer to their American dream. Filling out the application form is the first step - but what data must be included in the application, and what comes next?

The participation period of the DV program is determined each year by the US State Department. It usually starts in October and is limited to only a few weeks.

When will EDV Lottery 2025 Registration form Open ?

The EDV lottery registration form will open from the 4th October 2023 (17 Asoj 2081) and closes on 8th November 2023 (22 Kartik 2081). Similarly, DV results will be announced in May of the following year.

Is Nepal eligible for EDV 2025 ?

Yes, Last year 3808 Nepalese were selected for DV 2024. Similarly, The DV 2025 is coming on 4th October 2023 (17 Asoj 2081) and hence you can prepare to act. Each year almost 4000 Nepali people get selected for the DV lottery.

To apply for the Diversity Visa (DV) program, you must meet specific eligibility requirements:

Eligibility : DV online Application Requirements

- Applicants need to have a high school education(or equivalent), or two years of qualifying work experience to be suitable to enter the diversity visa program.

- You require a Valid Passport by your side to make sure the data on the form matches the details on the Passport. However, for 2024, Passports have not been kept mandatory.

- Make sure your country is listed in the List of qualified nations for the diversity visa.

- Other important documents required are Birth Certificates, Educational certificates, Working

- Experience certificates, Relationship documents, Medical documents, Court and Police records, etc. All these documents need to be presented during the interview in Original.

- X-rays and Vaccination records are mandatory as well.

- The Photo used for the earlier DV lottery shall not be suitable for this year’s lottery.

- All the applications before and after the mentioned duration shall be deemed ineligible.

- Applicants with multiple entries shall be disqualified, as directed by the United States government.

EDV Photo Requirements

Dimensions – 2×2 inches ( 51×51 mm) (600×600 – 1200×1200 pixels)

Format – JPGs up to 240 KB

Age of the Photo – within the last 6 months

Background – plain white or off-white

Basics – Neutral facial expression, Daily wear, Eyes open, Facing Camera

Don’t – Wear accessories, head covering, Eyeglasses

Rules & Regulations for DV Lottery Registration

- The US Embassy does not email the selected candidates nor will it publicize a list of selectees. You need to check EDV result by yourself.

- The emails arrive only for the instructions to follow for the Interview process.

- The email provides instructions to log into the Status Check portal and retrieve the date, time, and location of the interview.

- The initial selection does not guarantee approval for the Green card lottery, there are further conditions to be fulfilled.

- After a series of steps to complete, a consular interview determines if you will receive a visa.

- Undisclosing any Family member in the form shall get disqualified. Family members refer to Spouses and Children below 21.

- Kentucky Consular Center(KCC) destroys all correspondence received by mail.

- A non-refundable Diversity Visa fee of $330 is mandatory during the interview process.

- If selected, Winners shall receive a Sealed Immigrant Packet containing immigration documents.

- The visa makes you a Lawful Permanent Resident or an American green card holder and will be permitted to work and live permanently in the United States.

- There can be additional eligibility criteria for the Green Cards.

Applying for DV 2025 Online

Now, let's walk through the process of applying for the DV 2025 online:

I. First of all, Visit the official DV program website at dvprogram.state.gov

Apply Now

II. Click on "Begin Entry" to start your application.

III. Provide accurate personal information, including your full name, date of birth, birthplace (city and country), and nationality. Make sure to enter this information exactly as it appears on your official documents, such as your passport or national ID.


Date of Birth : Enter your date of birth, the same as in your official document (Citizenship or Passport).
Birth City : the citry where you were born, You can leave blank or write if you know your Birth City. This is optional.
Country : Write your birth country (Nationality).

IV. Country of Eligibility :
As stated in the requirement above, The Diversity Program is not eligible for all the country. Some nationalities are not eligible for DV Lottery. But if you are Nepalese national, then you can simply choose Yes.

V. Mailing Address :
Address Line 1: Tole name, ward name, and number (Eg. Balkot – 15)

City: Your City’s name (Eg. Ilam)

District/Province: Choose yours (Eg. Ilam District / Koshi Province)

Postal Code: Search google for your City or District Postal Code (Eg. 3800)

Country: Select your country name (Eg. Nepal)

If you in foreign country away from your homecountry then enter your current address and country.

VI. Contact Details
You need to submit your contact details like Phone Number which is optional, meaning you can leave it blank & Email address, which is important.

VII. Education Qualification
According to the U.S. Department of State Bureau of Consular Affairs, you should pass at least High school or have work experience, check out more information here.

VIII. Marital Status
You need to choose your marital status for DV program from the list below:
- Unmarried
- Married and my Spouse is not a US citizen
- Married and my spouse is a Us citizen
- Divorced
- Widowed
- Legally Separated

IX. Submitting :
If you have children, write the number of children (All of them) and click on continue to proceed. After clicking continue, you will be taken to another page where you will be asked to check all your information, make no mistakes, and check for errors. If you do not find any, then click on submit.

X. Saving Confirmation Number :
Print, Take picture, note it down or simply copy confirmation number save it on a computer. Be sure to keep your confirmation number in a safe place, as you will need it to check the status of your entry and to apply for a visa if you are selected. You cannot return to this page after closing the page. But you can recover dv lottery confirmation number using the email address you provided in the contact section.


Hence, You've successfully filled the DV Lottery 2025 online application. After submitting the application you will get your Confirmation number. Remember to check your status in May and be prepared for the subsequent steps if you are selected as a winner. Your American dream may be closer than you think. Good luck!

Check DV Lottery Results

The results of the DV Lottery are typically announced in May of the following year after the application period has closed. You can check your DV Lottery entrant status on the official DV program website dvprogram.state.gov during this time.

Remember, the US authorities do not send out notifications to selected candidates. It is your responsibility to check your status and follow the instructions provided for the next steps.

Winning the DV Lottery : What Comes Next

If you are selected as a DV Lottery winner, congratulations! Here's what you can expect in the next phases of the process:

- Filling Out the DS-260 Form
After winning the DV Lottery, you must complete the DS-260 form, where you will provide extensive personal information about yourself and your family members, including your spouse and any children under 21 years of age.
- Preparing for the Interview
You will be scheduled for a visa interview at a US consulate or embassy, usually in your home country. Approximately 14 days before your interview, you will need to undergo a medical examination conducted by a local panel physician, which usually takes 1-2 hours.
- Required Documents
You will need to bring various documents to your interview, including a copy of the biometrics page of your passport, your birth certificate or extract from birth registration, police certificates (if applicable), and other relevant documents.
- Additional Documents
Depending on your circumstances, you may need to provide additional documents such as marriage certificates, divorce certificates, military records, and more.
- Visa Interview
The interview itself typically lasts less than five minutes and is conducted by a consular officer. The focus is on verifying the submitted documents and ensuring that you meet the eligibility criteria.
- Green Card Activation
To activate your Green Card, you must travel to the United States within six months after your medical examination. There is no required length of stay, so you can plan your trip accordingly.
- Green Card Delivery
Approximately 3 to 6 months after your activation trip to the US, your Green Card will be sent to the US address you provided in your form. It's crucial to note that the US authorities will only send your Green Card to an address within the USA.
- Timing for Immigration
While winning the DV Lottery does not require immediate immigration, it's essential to remember that the US authorities expect DV Lottery winners to have an intention to immigrate. Leaving the USA for more than 12 months after receiving your Green Card could lead to complications.

Pursuing Your American Dream

The Diversity Visa Program offers a unique and valuable opportunity for individuals from eligible countries to achieve their American dreams by obtaining a Green Card and becoming lawful permanent residents of the United States. To maximize your chances of success, ensure that you meet the eligibility requirements, submit an accurate application, and follow the process diligently.

Conclusion about EDV

EDV stands for Electronic Diversity Visa, offered by the US each year. It is the only US immigrant Visa that is Free and Easy to apply for. The State Government also encourages you to apply for EDV 2024 by yourself or at least in your presence for the correct data input. As the name suggests, this Visa can only be applied for electronically.

Nepal is on the list of selected countries eligible for DV 2024. Last year 3808 Nepalese were selected for DV 2023. The DV 2024 is coming soon and hence you can prepare to act. Each year almost 4000 Nepali people get selected for the DV lottery.

This Visa offered by the US Department Of State grants the privilege to live and work in America holding a Green Card as a lawful resident of the US. The Visa was initiated in the fiscal year 1987 to preserve the Cultural Diversity of the US as per Section 203(c) of the Immigration and Nationality Act (INA).

FAQ : Electronic Diversity Visa Lottery Program Questions

How can I apply for the 2025 DV lottery?

Visit www.dvprogram.state.gov and follow the steps to fill out the DV form 2025. Visit the EDV lottery blog for detailed procedures.

What is the Photo Size for DV Lottery?

Your photo size for DV Lottery should be 600×600 pixels which is Square in shape.

Are there changes in the application form for EDV Lottery this year?

Passports have not been made mandatory and Citizenship can be used for filling out the DV form.

Why is the DV Lottery website not working?

Sometimes, EDV Lottery website might not work during the application period because of high traffic.

Can we fill out the DV application form offline?

As the name suggests, it is an Electronic visa type. Hence, the DV application is only available with the digital option.

When will EDV Lottery 2025 close?

The process of EDV 2025 shall last till 08th November 2023 (22 Kartik 2081).

How can I increase my chances of winning the DV lottery?

There’s no such factor that really affects the probability of winning a DV lottery. Simply, filling out the DV registration form accurately can increase your chances of winning the DV lottery visas.

Which countries are eligible for DV Lottery 2025?

Countries like Nepal, Afghanistan, Bhutan, Israel, Japan, Kuwait, Malaysia, Maldives, Qatar, Singapore, Sri Lanka, Taiwan, Thailand, etc are allowed to apply for DV this year.
