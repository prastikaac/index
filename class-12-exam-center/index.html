NEB Class 12 Exam Center 2080

NEB
Baisak 15, 2080   Prasiddha Acharya 3245

NEB has provided Class 12 Exam Center Lists Of All Over Nepal

The National Examination Board Examination Control Office has recently announced that there will be 1,453 examination centers for Class 12 students who will take the exam starting from 2080 Baishakh 26.

When will be Class 12 Board exams started?

National examination board (NEB) has published a notice of starting the Class 12 Board exams from 26 th of Baisak, 2080.

Changes in the Model Question Papers

The Examination Control Office is making some changes in the model question papers of some subjects, as per the suggestions of students and teachers. As per the recommendation, alternative questions should be kept in some subjects that were not kept with 'or'. The board is publishing the model question paper with the suggested changes

Answer sheets sent to Remote Districts

According to exam controller Krishna Prasad Sharma, the answer sheets of the students have already reached the examination centers in remote districts. Due to the ongoing COVID-19 pandemic, the board has stopped publishing question papers from the academic session 2077. The question paper sent by the examination board through online mode has to be printed by the examination center itself.

Participants in the Examination

Around 4,67,000 examinees will participate in the examination, according to the Examination Control Office. The board has taken every possible measure to ensure that the examination is conducted smoothly and without any hassle for the students.
